Short version: Some links on AI Pickz are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase or sign up, I may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. That never changes what I recommend.

What affiliate links are

When you click an affiliate link and buy a subscription, product, or service, the merchant may pay AI Pickz a referral fee. The price you pay is the same — the commission comes from the vendor, not from you.

Most affiliate links on this site go through networks such as AWIN or direct partner programs. Those networks may use cookies to attribute the referral. I do not control how merchants store that data.

How we choose what to recommend

  • Recommendations are based on fit for a specific use case — not commission rate.
  • If a tool is overpriced or underwhelming, I say so, even when an affiliate link exists.
  • I do not accept payment for coverage, sponsored rankings, or positive reviews.
  • Articles label affiliate relationships at the top and again near relevant links where practical.

How guides are built

Most articles on AI Pickz are integration-first guides: I compare official pricing and documentation (checked on the publish date) with recent community reports, then add a clear verdict. Some articles include hands-on spot-checks or longer tests — those sections say so explicitly.

I do not claim to have long-tested every tool in every comparison. When I haven't, the article says how it was built. See About for the full editorial process.

AI Pickz may display third-party ads (for example, through Google AdSense). Ads are separate from affiliate links. Ad placement does not determine which tools I recommend in editorial content.
