Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. What we collect

Anonymous usage data

We use GoatCounter, a privacy-first analytics tool, to collect anonymized page view data. This includes:

  • Page URL and title
  • Referrer (where you came from)
  • Screen size and browser type
  • Country-level location (not precise)

No cookies are used for analytics. GoatCounter does not track individual users across sessions, and we don't use Google Analytics or any ad tracking.

Contact form submissions

If you submit our contact form, we receive your name and email address. We use these only to respond to your message. We don't add them to any marketing list, and we don't share them with third parties.

2. What we don't collect

  • No cookies for tracking or advertising
  • No personal identification data beyond what you voluntarily provide
  • No login or account system (no passwords, no user profiles)
  • No social media tracking pixels

3. Affiliate links

Some links on this site are affiliate links (primarily through AWIN). When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, the merchant knows you came from AI Pickz. We may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Affiliate networks may place a cookie to track the referral. These cookies are set by the affiliate network, not by us. We have no control over how they handle this data.

4. Third-party services

  • GoatCounter — Privacy-friendly analytics (EU-based, GDPR-compliant)
  • FormSubmit — Contact form processing (we receive the data via email only)
  • Vercel — Site hosting (standard server logs, no additional tracking)
  • AWIN — Affiliate network (cookie-based referral tracking)

5. Your rights

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us and we'll respond within 30 days.

6. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. We'll also note significant changes in our blog.
