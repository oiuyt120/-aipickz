What's AI Pickz?

We buy AI tools, test them for real, and tell you which ones are worth your money. No affiliate-fluffed rankings, no sponsored top-10s.

Why this site exists

There are hundreds of AI tools launching every month. Most review sites rank by affiliate commission, not by actual quality. We got tired of reading "this tool is amazing" only to find it couldn't do the one thing we needed.

So we started AI Pickz — a place where every review comes from someone who actually used the tool for its intended purpose, not someone who read the landing page.

How we review

  • We buy or sign up ourselves. No free review units, no sponsored access.
  • We test for a minimum of 3 days. Longer for complex tools like coding assistants or video editors.
  • We compare across real scenarios. Not just benchmarks — actual workflows.
  • We tell you what we'd pay. And what we wouldn't.

Who this is for

You're a freelancer, a creator, a developer, or someone who just wants to work smarter. You don't have time to test 10 AI writing tools to find the one that doesn't sound robotic. You want someone to do the grunt work and hand you a straight answer.

That's us.

What we cover

  • Reviews: Deep dives into individual AI tools — writing, coding, image, voice, video, productivity.
  • Comparisons: Head-to-head tests so you can pick the right tool for your specific use case.
  • Weekly Picks: Curated roundups of tools we actually use and recommend.

Our promise

Every review on this site is independent. We don't charge for coverage. We don't remove negative findings in exchange for affiliate revenue. If a tool is bad, we say so.

Some links on this site are affiliate links — if you buy through them, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. But that never influences what we write. We'd rather lose a commission than recommend a tool we don't believe in.

Got a tool you want us to test? Send it in. If it's legit and our audience would benefit, we'll put it through the wringer.
